Welcome to Food Waste Rescue

Our mission is to reduce food waste and help those in need. Join us in rescuing surplus food and getting it to communities that need it most.

How You Can Help

  • Donate surplus food from your business or home.
  • Volunteer with food collection and distribution.
  • Partner with local restaurants, grocers, or caterers.
  • Host community food drives and fundraisers.
  • Spread awareness about food waste and food security.
