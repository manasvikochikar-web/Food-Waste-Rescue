Welcome to FoodWasteRescue.com

Our mission is to reduce food waste and help those in need. Join us in our efforts to rescue surplus food and distribute it to communities that need it most.


How You Can Help

  • Donate surplus food from your business or home.
  • Volunteer your time to help with food collection and distribution.
  • Partner with us as a local restaurant, grocery store, or caterer to set up recurring food rescues.
  • Host a community food drive or fundraiser to help support our logistics and storage facilities.
  • Advocate for policy changes and community initiatives that support food security and reduce landfill waste.
  • Make a financial contribution to help us expand our reach and keep our delivery vehicles running.
  • Practice smart shopping and meal planning at home to reduce waste at the source.
  • Spread awareness about food waste and its impact on the environment.

Get Involved

Sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on our latest initiatives and events. Follow us on social media to see how you can make a difference in your community.
